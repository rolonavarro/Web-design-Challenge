Stock Reaction to The Pandemic

Stocks

In this project I wanted to see the price change of different stocks, and how people react to different situations. During this pandemic there are news, comming up all the time that have a big impact the price of the stock. I looked at Apple stock, at S&P 500 Index, and at the price of oil. These 3 elements are a good representation of the economy and they respond different to news. Overall, it is clear that when an economy enters a recession their prices will be hit in a negative way.
