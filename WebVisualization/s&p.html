This graph perfectly represents how much volatility and speculation there is in the market. On average people hold a stock for much less time compared to 10 or 20 years ago. Oil Price

One of the sectors that I like to keep up with is oil. Russia and Saudia Arabia were violating OPEC rules, so I thought it was interesting how the price of oil moved throughout this conflict. By mid-April, all of the countries participating in the OPEC agreed to significantly reduce their oil production to increase the oil prices as demand decreased significantly. Even with that agreement, the oil price hit a historic low.
